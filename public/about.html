About our foundation

JSSWM is a non-profitable voluntary organization serving the needy, focusing on communities such as Slum, Leper, and Tribal-Communities living in poverty and injustice to become self-sufficient and bring lasting change over the past decade.

Hanoch Putra, Founder and President, and Jacob Sundar Putra, Vice-President, work through a committee. Every month, a council meeting is held to finalize charity activities through resolutions passed by the committee.

Vision: Know Jesus and Make Him Known with Love and Care. Serving all the needy with no caste or creed.

“I was hungry and you gave me something to eat, I was thirsty and you gave me something to drink, I was a stranger and you invited me in, I needed clothes and you clothed me, I was sick and you looked after me, I was in prison and you came to visit me” [Matthew 25:35-37]

The total population of India is 150 crore [1.5 billion]. Christians account for 3.22 crore [32 million]. The goal of JSSWM is to reach the unreached.

  • 2. Education
