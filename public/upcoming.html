Upcoming Project Requirements

Requirements

1. Leprosy Hospital

Leprosy has left many individuals marginalized and isolated from mainstream healthcare. The establishment of a Leprosy Hospital aims to address this gap, providing a holistic approach to treatment, rehabilitation, and community reintegration.

Mission: Our mission is to create a center of excellence that goes beyond medical care, focusing on the overall well-being of individuals affected by leprosy. This includes:

  • Medical Treatment: State-of-the-art facilities staffed by experienced healthcare professionals, offering specialized treatment and rehabilitation services.
  • Education and Awareness: Conducting educational programs to dispel myths and reduce the social stigma associated with leprosy, fostering a more inclusive and understanding society.
  • Community Integration: Implementing initiatives that support the reintegration of individuals cured of leprosy into their communities, ensuring they are not subjected to discrimination.

2. Orphanages

Background: There is a pressing need for a well-organized and caring facility to cater to the needs of orphaned children in our community. The establishment of an orphanage is essential to address the challenges faced by these vulnerable children and provide them with the care, education, and guidance necessary for their holistic development.

Mission: Our mission is to create a loving and secure haven for orphaned children, offering them the following:

  • Residential Quarters: Well-equipped living spaces that prioritize the comfort and safety of the children.
  • Educational Center: Classrooms and educational resources to facilitate learning and intellectual development.
  • Recreational Areas: Playgrounds and recreational spaces to encourage physical activity and social interaction among the children.

3. Church Constructions

We aim to build a simple and functional church to accommodate our growing community. Create a welcoming space for worship, fellowship, and community outreach.

The church is a vital space to share the good news in unreached areas. In the future, we aim to build 100 churches in these areas.
